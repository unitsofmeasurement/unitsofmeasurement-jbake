Leonardo and Werner as well as Bruno representing SouJava (with members especially Otavio involved as EG Member via Adopt-a-JSR) met for the first EG F2F in 2015, pretty much one year after the "Kick-off" presentation at Oracle last January. <a href="https://jcp.org/aboutJava/communityprocess/ec-public/materials/2015-01-1314/January-2015-Public-Minutes.html">January 2015 JCP EC F2F</a> at Freescale in San Jose, California.

Leonardo and Werner reported on the state of JSR 363, the recently started EDR and plans for next stages of the JSR.
